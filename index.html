About Us

Empowering Afghan Businesses Through Digital Innovation

IDEA AFG is a leading ICT solutions provider dedicated to transforming businesses through cutting-edge technology. We specialize in custom software development, system integration, and digital transformation services.

IDEA AFG is a premier ICT solutions company based in Herat, Afghanistan. We provide comprehensive technology services including software development, accounting systems, mobile applications, and cloud solutions.

Our experienced team combines local market knowledge with international best practices to deliver reliable, scalable, and secure digital solutions that drive business growth and operational efficiency.

To become Afghanistan's leading ICT solutions provider, empowering businesses with innovative technology that drives digital transformation and economic growth.

We envision a future where every Afghan business has access to world-class technology solutions that enhance productivity, improve customer experiences, and create sustainable competitive advantages.

Founded with a mission to bridge the technology gap in Afghanistan, IDEA AFG has been serving businesses across various industries with innovative ICT solutions and exceptional customer service.

From our headquarters in Herat, we have successfully delivered numerous projects ranging from small business applications to enterprise-level systems, establishing ourselves as a trusted technology partner.
